Instagram: Click here...

Instagram is a visual-centric platform that focuses on sharing photos and videos. Its square-shaped logo is instantly recognizable. Instagram allows users to capture moments, apply filters, and share them with their followers. It emphasizes visual storytelling and provides features like Stories, IGTV, and Reels to enhance creativity.

Quora: Click here...

Quora stands out as a question-and-answer platform, fostering knowledge-sharing and discussions. Its logo features a blue "Q" inside a speech bubble. Quora enables users to ask questions and receive answers from a diverse community of experts and enthusiasts.

Twitter: Click here...

Twitter is a microblogging platform recognized by its iconic blue bird logo. It allows users to share short messages called "tweets" of up to 280 characters. Twitter emphasizes real-time conversations, news updates, and trends. Users can follow others, retweet, like, and reply to tweets.
